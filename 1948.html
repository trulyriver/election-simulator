Harry S. Truman

Party: Democrat

Thomas E. Dewey

Party: Republican

Bio: Former New York Governor, again Republican nominee, running on efficient government and a strong anti-communist stance.

Strom Thurmond

Party: Dixiecrat (States' Rights Democratic Party)

Bio: South Carolina governor, running on a segregationist platform.

Henry Wallace

Party: Progressive

Bio: Former vice president advocating civil rights, expanded social programs, and peaceful relations with the Soviet Union.
