Welcome to the Election of 1796

The year is 1796. George Washington, the hero of the American Revolution and the nation’s first president, has decided not to seek a third term.

For the first time, you—as a voting American citizen—must choose between rival candidates in a contested presidential election.

The future of the young republic is in your hands.
